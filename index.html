About Me

My research interests lie at the intersection of generative modeling and efficient deep learning. I am currently exploring flow-matching and rectified-flow methods for image generation, as well as large language model compression, representation learning, and knowledge transfer.

Before joining Nanjing University of Science and Technology, I received my bachelor's degree in Data Science and Big Data Technology from Henan University.

Projects

Ongoing Research

Large Language Models

AbilityBridge

AbilityBridge studies ability-specific subspace transport for compressing capabilities from larger language models into smaller models. The project investigates ability-aware optimal transport, sparse representations, representation distillation, and capability-preserving compression.

Ongoing

Generative Modeling

VRAIFLOW

VRAIFLOW is an ongoing research project on image generation with flow matching and rectified-flow models, with an emphasis on efficient and high-quality generative modeling.
